PROCESS

1. CONTACT

Contact me via e-mail so that we can plan a call to discuss your needs. Feel free to include pictures of projects of mine (or not) that you would like to replicate!

2. DISCUSSION

We talk about what you need and when you need it, we share ideas and I make sure I understand the project perfectly so that I can get to work on solid grounds.

3. DESIGN

I get to work on your project, sending you a number of iterations of the design, improving it with your insights and making it perfect for final delivery.

4. DELIVERY

After the payment, I send the final digital files to you. These could be anything: JPG, PNG, PSD, AI, EPS, PDF, OBJ, STL, PPT... You get the point!

5. REVISIONS

You have two weeks to ask me for a final revision if something comes up that we didn't see before, free of charge!
